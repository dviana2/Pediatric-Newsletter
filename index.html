Welcome to PAA's Quarterly Newsletter!

Our mission at Pediatric Associates is to provide comprehensive health care for children from birth through young adulthood. We strive to create the most accessible, compassionate and evidence-based pediatric medical home. That means our practice team is always learning, changing and improving to best meet the needs of the children in our families in our care.

To better accomplish our goals, we are excited to launch this new, quarterly newsletter. Through these updates, we hope to keep our families well-informed about the newest updates in our practice and children's health.

Should Parents Worry About
Monkeypox?

The risk of children getting infected with monkeypox virus is very, very low. As of August 3, 2022, < 0.1% of all cases have been in children.

Learn more about what this virus means for children.
